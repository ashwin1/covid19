About Us

The website is built and maintained by a team of professors and students of the University of Texas at Dallas. We hope to simplify the pandemic by providing information about the corona spread and the resources that are available in and around Texas. U.S. health officials continue to monitor for coronavirus cases in the United States. As of August 3, more than 4 million cases have been confirmed in the U.S., based on NBC News' count. There have been more than 150,000 deaths.

The website is developed by University of Texas at Dallas team.
